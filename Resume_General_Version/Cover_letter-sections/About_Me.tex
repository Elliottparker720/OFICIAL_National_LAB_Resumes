\lettersection{About Me}

I’m applying for the Associate Mechanical Engineer position with the LCLS team. My work at SLAC has centered on the development, integration, and restoration of complex accelerator systems, including vacuum, electrical, and cooling subsystems supporting beamline operation. Most recently, I led the recommissioning of a dormant positron beamline and a bunch length diagnostic system, coordinating across engineering, technical, and operations teams to return both systems to reliable use. While my role has included data analysis and diagnostics, the core of my experience is hands-on systems engineering—working directly with hardware, troubleshooting integrated systems, and improving reliability and maintainability in high-performance environments.



\lettersection{Why I Fit}

I’m particularly interested in this role because it aligns with the direction my work has naturally taken: contributing to the design, integration, and operation of advanced experimental systems. My background with high-vacuum environments, cross-disciplinary system integration, and engineering support for scientific instrumentation would allow me to contribute quickly to LCLS instrument development and upgrades
